About Me

Hello, my name is Habtamu Zenebe,

and I am a Computer Science student at Wachemo University with a strong passion for visual storytelling and creativity.

Over the years, I've honed my skills in both video editing and graphic design, developing a keen eye for detail and a dedication to delivering high-quality, engaging content.

As a video editor, I focus on crafting compelling narratives that captivate viewers and convey messages effectively. My approach combines technical precision with a creative touch, ensuring that each project I work on is visually striking and communicates the intended story.

I am skilled in using industry-standard tools and software to create polished and professional video content that resonates with audiences. In graphic design, I apply principles of layout, color theory, and typography to produce cohesive and impactful designs. I enjoy experimenting with visual elements to bring fresh ideas to life, whether it’s creating eye-catching graphics, designing logos, or developing promotional materials.

My design process is rooted in understanding the client’s vision and transforming it into meaningful, aesthetically pleasing visuals. Balancing my studies with my creative pursuits has taught me to manage projects efficiently and adapt to new challenges, helping me continuously grow as a multimedia creator.

I’m excited to expand my portfolio and collaborate with clients who value creativity and innovation. Explore my portfolio to see how my experience in video editing and graphic design can bring a unique and professional edge to your projects. Let’s create something memorable together!
